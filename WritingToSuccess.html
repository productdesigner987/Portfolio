The hardest thing writers experience are the ideas. Good ideas are really hard to come to mind.

You could sit there all day long, thinking, but all the ideas you get aren't that great.

Many writers have struggled with this for a long time. I can give you a few tips about ideas.

  • Think about a person, place, or feeling. Think about your surroundings. Write a few stories about those
  • Take any struggle in your life and turn it into a story
  • Think about things you want and how life would be with it
